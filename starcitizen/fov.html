• FOV (Advanced)

    • Navigate to your Star Citizen "Live" (or "PTU") folder
    • Create a file named "user.cfg"
    • Open "user.cfg" in Notepad
    • Insert:
    r_width = 2560
    r_height = 1080
    • Start Star Citizen
    • Set FOV to 115
    • Change resolution back to your preferred resolution
    • Quit Star Citizen
    • Reopen "user.cfg"
    • Replace the above with:
    -- r_width = 2560
    -- r_height = 1080
    • Enjoy your new FOV!
